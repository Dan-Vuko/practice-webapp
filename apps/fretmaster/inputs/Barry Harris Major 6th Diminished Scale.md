# Barry Harris Major 6th Diminished Scale
## Three Parent Scale Systems in One 8-Note Structure

### Core Scale Structure

**C Major 6 Diminished:** C D E F G G# A B
**Formula:** 1 2 3 4 5 ♭6 6 7

## System 1: Major Scale Modes (Drop G#)

Remove G# to get: **C D E F G A B** (C Major Scale)

**Mode 1 - Ionian (Major Scale)**
Intervals: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
Example: C D E F G A B

**Mode 2 - Dorian**
Intervals: 1 2 b3 4 5 6 b7
Example: D E F G A B C

**Mode 3 - Phrygian**
Intervals: 1 b2 b3 4 5 b6 b7
Example: E F G A B C D

**Mode 4 - Lydian**
Intervals: 1 2 3 #4 5 6 7
Example: F G A B C D E

**Mode 5 - Mixolydian**
Intervals: 1 2 3 4 5 6 b7
Example: G A B C D E F

**Mode 6 - Aeolian (Natural Minor)**
Intervals: 1 2 b3 4 5 b6 b7
Example: A B C D E F G

**Mode 7 - Locrian**
Intervals: 1 b2 b3 4 b5 b6 b7
Example: B C D E F G A

## System 2: Harmonic Minor Modes (Drop G)

Remove G to get: **A B C D E F G#** (A Harmonic Minor)

**Mode 1 - Harmonic Minor**
Intervals: 1 2 b3 4 5 b6 7
Example: A B C D E F G#

**Mode 2 - Locrian ♮6**
Intervals: 1 b2 b3 4 b5 6 b7
Example: B C D E F G# A

**Mode 3 - Ionian #5 (Augmented Major)**
Intervals: 1 2 3 4 #5 6 7
Example: C D E F G# A B

**Mode 4 - Dorian #4 (Romanian Minor)**
Intervals: 1 2 b3 #4 5 6 b7
Example: D E F G# A B C

**Mode 5 - Phrygian Dominant (Mixolydian b2 b6)**
Intervals: 1 b2 3 4 5 b6 b7
Example: E F G# A B C D

**Mode 6 - Lydian #2**
Intervals: 1 #2 3 #4 5 6 7
Example: F G# A B C D E

**Mode 7 - Super Locrian bb7 (Altered Diminished)**
Intervals: 1 b2 b3 b4 b5 b6 bb7
Example: G# A B C D E F

## System 3: Harmonic Major Modes (Drop A)

Remove A to get: **C D E F G A♭ B** (C Harmonic Major)

**Mode 1 - Harmonic Major (Ionian b6)**
Intervals: 1 2 3 4 5 b6 7
Example: C D E F G Ab B

**Mode 2 - Dorian b5 (Locrian ♮2 ♮6)**
Intervals: 1 2 b3 4 b5 6 b7
Example: D E F G Ab B C

**Mode 3 - Phrygian b4**
Intervals: 1 b2 b3 b4 5 b6 b7
Example: E F G Ab B C D

**Mode 4 - Lydian b3 (Lydian Diminished)**
Intervals: 1 2 b3 #4 5 6 7
Example: F G Ab B C D E

**Mode 5 - Mixolydian b9 (Mixolydian b2)**
Intervals: 1 b2 3 4 5 6 b7
Example: G Ab B C D E F

**Mode 6 - Lydian Augmented #2**
Intervals: 1 #2 3 #4 #5 6 7
Example: Ab B C D E F G

**Mode 7 - Locrian bb7**
Intervals: 1 b2 b3 4 b5 b6 bb7
Example: B C D E F G Ab

## Summary and Implications

The Barry Harris Major 6th Diminished scale contains 21 different 7-note scales by removing one of three notes:

- **Drop G#** → All 7 Major Scale modes
- **Drop G** → All 7 Harmonic Minor modes
- **Drop A** → All 7 Harmonic Major modes

This creates an efficient superstructure for improvisation and composition across multiple harmonic contexts. The single 8-note parent scale provides access to three complete modal systems, making it highly versatile for both tonal and modal applications.
